Experience Summer in Pure Michigan

With long days of comfortable temperatures and the nations longest freshwater coastline, Michigan is a summer destination like no other. Our two peninsulas are home to more than 11,000 lakes, forested state parks, spectacular dark skies and vibrant urban landscapes that set a scene of both peaceful tranquility and adventure for an endless list of summer activities.

About

If you seek a pleasant peninsula, look about you. Michigans official state motto holds many truths our state is a land of great splendors. Full of natural beauty, historical importance, and a talented, resourceful population, Michigan has it all. Our state services support residents, businesses, and visitors to the Great Lakes state.
