When you visit our website, we may store or collect information about your visit in the form of cookies. This information is mostly used to make the site work. Your Cookie Settings

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are needed for the website to work and cannot be turned off. They usually happen when you do things like add an item to your bag or set a language preference. Although you can block or set alerts for these cookies, parts of the site will stop working. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

Targeting Cookies

These cookies are usually set by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites. Although they dont store your personal information directly, they do identify your browser and device. If you dont allow these cookies, you will see less targeted advertising.

Functional Cookies

With these cookies, the website can offer enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third party providers whose services we use. By allowing these cookies, you get content tailored to you and a more customized site experience.

Performance Cookies

These cookies let us measure our websites performance. They count visits and traffic sources, telling us which pages are popular and how visitors move around the site. This information is aggregated, making it anonymous. By allowing these cookies, you help us understand where and when errors occur on the site. This helps us improve the site experience.
